# Company Remote Work Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work at KnowledgeHub Inc.

## 2. Eligibility
All employees in the Engineering and Product departments are eligible for remote work up to 3 days a week.

## 3. Core Hours
Employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

## 4. Equipment
The company provides a laptop and a headset. Employees are responsible for their internet connection.
